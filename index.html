friends & favs

If you're as lazy as I am, you want other people to create your playlist. Enter friends & favs. Connect with SoundCloud and enjoy a playlist made out of your followings' favorites.


This website was created by Nikolaus Piccolotto and is loosely based on favematic by Allana Scott. Made with baselinecss, jQuery and the SoundCloud API. Check out the code at GitHub, if you're into it.
